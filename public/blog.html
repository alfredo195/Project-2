When the first user Clicks "Ready for battle" it takes the user to the "create a game" page. First player clicks "new game" which brings them to "battle page" where Player 1 is waiting for player 2 to join. Player 2 enters name and the room id that is displayed on the first window. Player 1 will provide provide room id for player 2 to join. Last thing to do on this page is click on "join game". Once player 2 clicks "join game" they will be brought to the battle page where player 1 is waiting, which will be displayed at the top of the "battle page. The battle zone is a turn based arena where players take turns attacking one another by clicking the attack button. With each attack the opponents health points (hp) go down, until one wins the game. =======

  • Make a username using the navbar link
  • Click on "Ready to battle" and select your username from the dropdown
  • Click on the "New Game" button to create a room
